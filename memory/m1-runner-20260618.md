M1 runner built at `scripts/m1_framework_validation.py`. Pipeline: gold annotation (SciSciPaper → gold dict) → LLM component extraction (6 components as JSON) → dimension scoring (D1-D5 × 6 components) → 4 M1 validation tests.

**How to apply**:

- `python scripts/m1_framework_validation.py --papers wu2019_disruption --model qwen3-32b` for single paper
