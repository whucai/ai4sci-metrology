# Limited Generalizability in Argument Mining: State-Of-The-Art Models Learn Datasets, Not Arguments

## One-line thesis
Large-scale re-evaluation of 4 transformers on 17 English sentence-level datasets; models rely on lexical shortcuts/content words rather than true argument understanding; contrastive pre-training + joint training improves robustness.
